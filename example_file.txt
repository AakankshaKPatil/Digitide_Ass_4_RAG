The Taj Mahal is a white marble mausoleum located in Agra, India. 
It was commissioned in 1632 by the Mughal emperor Shah Jahan 
to house the tomb of his favorite wife, Mumtaz Mahal. 
The Taj Mahal also houses the tomb of Shah Jahan himself. 
The construction project employed thousands of artisans and craftsmen 
and took about 20 years to complete. It is considered one of the 
most beautiful architectural achievements in the world and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. 
Visitors from around the globe travel to India to see the Taj Mahal each year.
